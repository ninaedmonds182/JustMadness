🎉 What is Dog's Bakery?

Dog's Bakery is a secret-agent baking party where children become undercover agents for a day. They solve codes, complete silly challenges, and unlock the ingredients to bake their own birthday cake — all led by themselves, while you play the role of their (slightly confused) assistant.

📋 How to set up — follow these screens in order:

1/9 Children & Teams — Enter names, birthday child, and number of teams. Codes are generated automatically.
2/9 Gender & Photos — Set each child's gender so the right baker dog is assigned. Photos are optional.
3/9 Game Codes — Download and print the code sheet. These are hidden around the room before the party.
4/9 Games & Levels — Choose 🟢 Simple, 🤪 Mad or 🤯 Bonkers for each of the 5 games. Enable Cake Fight if you dare.
5/9 Cake Recipes — Select which cakes to bake. Ingredients reveal one by one as agents complete games.
6/9 Downloads & Print — Wristbands, codes, games guide, recipes and certificates all in one place.
7/9 Shopping List — Combined ingredient quantities + game equipment for your chosen levels. Tick off as you shop.
8/9 Certificates — Preview each child's certificate. Download and print before the party.
9/9 Lock & Launch — Pick the party leader, tick the checklist, then lock and hand the device to the kids.
⏱️ Setup time: Around 30–45 minutes for physical prep (hiding codes, setting up games) plus 10–15 minutes on this device. Do the device setup first, then use the shopping list and games guide to prepare the room.
💡 Tip: Everything saves automatically. You can close the app and come back any time — all your setup will be here waiting.

🔑 Game Codes
These are the secret codes agents must find and enter during the mission to unlock baking ingredients.
🔐 How codes work:
Each game has its own set of real codes hidden somewhere in the room — agents must find them to unlock the next ingredient.
Game 3 also has dummy codes to trick the agents — entering a dummy triggers a funny dog error!

📋 What you need to do:
1. Download the code sheet below and print it (or keep it on a second device).
2. Check the next screen (Setup 3/8) for full game instructions — it tells you exactly where and how to hide each code for every game and difficulty level.
3. Hide the codes before the party starts — the agents must not see them!

CAKE FIGHT!

Before the chaos begins — the OFFICIAL rules. Read them. Learn them. Try to follow them.

✅ YOU MAY:
🧽Use your sponge to apply cream — gently. Professor Baker approves of controlled squirting.
🍦Spray cream at torsos and arms only — the legal target zone.
😂Laugh uncontrollably. This is encouraged. In fact it is mandatory.
🤝Negotiate temporary alliances. Betrayals are also acceptable.
🏳️Surrender by waving a sponge above your head. No one will respect this. But try anyway.
❌ YOU MAY NOT:
🚫Aim at faces, eyes or hair. The assistant has already filled in the incident report form. Do not make it worse.
🍰Throw actual cake. That cake took 2 hours to make and deserves better. Eat it. Then fight.
👓Spray cream at anyone wearing glasses. Basic decency. Even in warfare.
🏃Chase anyone who says stop. If someone says stop — it stops. Full paws down.
📱Spray cream near phones, devices, or the assistant. The assistant is NOT a valid target. (They bought the cream. Show some respect.)
🐕Involve any actual dogs. They will eat all the cream and feel no shame about it.
🎒 Equipment Check
🧽 Sponges — 1 per player
🍦 Spray cream — 1 can per 2–3 children
🛁 Garden / easy-clean area strongly advised
👕 Old clothes on — or be prepared to explain this to the washing machine
On the count of 3... 🎂💥 LET THE CHAOS BEGIN!
