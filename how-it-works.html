How It Works

Everything you need to know before the madness begins.

Four steps. That's it.

Here's exactly what happens, start to finish.

1

Choose your mission ⏱ 2 minutes

Browse the missions and pick the one that suits your party. Currently Dog's Bakery is available — more on the way.

2

Personalise and choose your track ⏱ 10–15 minutes

Enter your access code, add the children's names, sort them into teams and pick your timeline: the 4-hour Full Baking Track — baking, challenges and decorating all included — or the quicker 3-hour Decorating-Only Track if you'd rather skip straight to the icing.

3

Prepare the challenges ⏱ 30–45 minutes

Follow your step-by-step setup guide. Hide the recipe codes, get the space ready and stage the challenges. Go Mild and you'll be done in 30. Go Bonkers and you'll want the full 45. The guide tells you exactly what to do — zero guesswork.

4

Hand it over ⏱ Instant

Give the device to the children. They're in charge now. You're their assistant — an important one, but an assistant nonetheless.

Then sit back and watch the children take over.

What happens during the mission?

A party with a beginning, a middle, and an ending they actually earned.

Here's what that looks like in practice:

Children arrive and immediately have a mission. They know what they're here to do.
A short video sets the scene — then the device goes down and the real chaos begins.
They work in teams, crack codes, run challenges and unlock each stage of the bake.
They bake real things, decorate real cakes, and judge each other with great seriousness.
They earn certificates with their actual names on. They feel like they genuinely achieved something.
They go home and tell their parents exactly what happened. In detail. For days.

You set the madness level. They run wild with it.
